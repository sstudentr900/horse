Equestrian Introduction

Our Surroundings

Magical Equestrian Center has the most natural environment for both riders and horses exercising in the most relaxed and comfortable state.

A comparable to top resorts environment

It is located in a forest of 400 meters above sea level in fresh weather with cool temperatures. It overlooks Guanyin Mountain from the sea and the mountains with sunset, colorful clouds, mountains, and dense fog ‚Äîall of which you can enjoy when riding.

Horses living in cool climate closest to that in Europe and America

With the cool climate of nature, the clean and fresh space reduces the presence of mosquitoes and flies, Many horses with skin diseases are recovered without any medication after coming here.

Spacious and comfortable first-class management and barn

The barn is 8 meters high, 4 meters long and 4 meters wide-- the most spacious and suitable in Taiwan. It is equipped with die-casting troughs, automatic water supply and ventilation systems.

Complete facilities planned for horses

The front and back rows distinguish between washing and hoisting horses to separate wet and dry areas. Medical lamps with the brightest light for veterinary treatment are equipped.

Ideal place to keep feed

Completely independent sealed feed room with built-in fan and padded wooden board is designed to avoid moisture and rats to provide the cleanest and ideal place to keep feed.

Space for saddlery

We offer a spacious and neat saddle storage space, and a large dehumidifier is installed to avoid leather saddles from mold due to the humid climate in Taiwan.
